How Our Products Are Manufactured

At Shambhuraavi Polyplast, we follow a structured, high-quality manufacturing process starting from recycled plastic material to finished packaging goods. With cutting-edge machinery like Monolayer Blown Film Plants and Bottom Seal Bag Machines, our production line ensures durability, cost-efficiency, and zero-defect output for every batch. All raw materials and outputs are quality tested and stored in our 20,000 sq. ft. facility before delivery.

1. Plastic Recycling

We begin with plastic scrap, recycled and converted into high-grade granules.

2. Film Extrusion

Granules are processed through monolayer blown film plants to form LDPE/HDPE films.

3. Cutting & Bag Making

Advanced Bottom Seal Bag Machines shape the films into bags, sheets, or rolls.

4. Quality Control

Every product passes through strict ISO 9001:2015-compliant checks.

5. Packaging & Storage

Approved goods are packed and moved to our 5,000 sq. ft. storage before dispatch.
