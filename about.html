About Us

Shambhuraavi Polyplast is led by Mr. Santosh Randive, Mr. Satish Gawade, and Mr. Shamkant Kale, bringing over 20 years of expertise in plastic manufacturing and packaging.

We specialize in flexible packaging films and plastic strap rolls, known for our quality, reliability, and precision. Our goal is to offer eco-friendly packaging solutions that meet the needs of modern industries.

  • Advanced manufacturing techniques
  • Consistent quality control
  • Timely delivery and client support

We proudly serve both Indian and multinational clients, delivering durable and innovative products like:

  • LDPE / HDPE Films
  • Shrink & Stretch Films
  • Air Bubble & Foam Sheets
  • Antistatic and VCI Bags
  • PP Strap Rolls and more
